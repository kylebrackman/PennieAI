## PennieAI

PennieAI is a tool for veterinarians to streamline their workflow and enhance patient care using AI technology.

PennieAI can help veterinarians by taking in an amalgamation of documents - emails, patient records, notes - and extracting relevant information to create a concise summary of the patient's history and current condition. This allows veterinarians to quickly understand the patient's background and make informed decisions about their care.
